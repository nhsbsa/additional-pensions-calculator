Additional Pension Calculator

Use this service to calculate your additional pension.

Information:

This service is only for active contributing members of the 2015 Pension scheme.

If your birthday is in the next 6 weeks, we advise you do the calculation and send it to your employer after your birthday.

Before you start

We'll ask you:

  • for your date of birth
  • for any previous additional pension contracts you may have
  • how much additional pension you would like
  • if you would like dependant cover
  • how your additional pension will be purchased

Make sure you read the additional pension factsheet before you complete the form.

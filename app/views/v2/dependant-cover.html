Dependant cover

Would you like to add dependant cover?

Dependant cover will provide benefits for your dependants in the event of your death.

What is dependant cover?

You can buy additional pension with cover for dependants. This could be your:

  • spouse
  • partner
  • dependant children

Each £250 of additional pension you buy also increases the adult dependant’s pension by £93.75 a year. For dependant children’s pension, the increase is £187.50 a year.

Adding dependant cover will increase the total cost of your additional pension purchase. The amount it increases by is affected by your age, the type of additional pension and the national pension age.

If you die within 12 months or less of buying additional pension with dependant cover:

  • your additional pension agreement will be cancelled
  • we will refund your one-off payment or any monthly payments you have made

If you die after the first 12 months of buying additional pension with dependant cover:

  • we will waive all future payments due after the date of death
  • we will pay you the full amount of additional pension you were buying

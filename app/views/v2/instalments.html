How do the monthly payments work?

You can select to make payments for 1 year, up to a maximum of 20 years. How many years you can select will depend on:

  • your age
  • how far away from the State Pension age (SPA) you are

For example, if you were born in 1995 and applied after your birthday in 2025, you could select 1 year to the maximum of 20 years. At age 30, you are 36 years away from the current SPA of 66

If you were born in 1975 and applied after your birthday in 2025, you could select 1 year to a maximum 16 years. At age 50, you are 16 years away from the current SPA of 66.

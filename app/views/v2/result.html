What you need to do next

If you’d like to buy your additional pension after you’ve checked your calculation, you need to:

  • select the download your form button to download an AP1 form
  • print off the form and complete all 3 sections in part A
  • send the form to your employer or pension officer within 6 weeks of today's date

You can find more information about completing your AP1 form in our additional pension factsheet (PDF: 211KB, opens in new tab).

It can take up to 6 weeks to process your application to buy additional pension.

If your birthday is in the next 6 weeks

You need to wait until after your birthday and complete a new calculation.

This is because your age affects the cost of buying additional pension. As it can take up to 6 weeks to process your application, you should not buy any additional pension until after your birthday.

What your employer will do

They’ll complete part B of the AP1 form and send us your application.

We aim to respond within 4 weeks of the date we receive this.

Downloading your additional pension application form

When you download your form, you'll need to print it off and fill it in.

Your form will open in a new tab as a PDF.

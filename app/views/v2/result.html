Next steps

Once you are happy with your calculation, you can print your AP1 form.

Make sure you:

  • read the additional pension factsheet before you complete the form
  • print out, fill in and send the form to your employer within 6 weeks of making the calculation

It will take up to 6 weeks to confirm your application for additional pension.

If your birthday is in the next 6 weeks, you should wait until after your birthday. Then you can do the calculation and send it to your employer.

Your NHS employer will complete their part of the AP1 form and forward your application to NHS Pensions.

We aim to respond within 4 weeks.

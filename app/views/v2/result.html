Next steps

Once you are happy with your calculation, make sure you:

  • read the additional pension factsheet before you complete the AP1 form
  • print out and fill in your partially completed form
  • send the form to your employer or Pension Officer within 6 weeks of making the calculation

It will take up to 6 weeks to confirm your application for additional pension.

If your birthday is in the next 6 weeks, you should wait until after your birthday. Then you can do the calculation and send it to your employer or pensions officer.

Your NHS employer will complete their part of the AP1 form and send your application to NHS Pensions.

We aim to respond within 4 weeks of receiving your application from your employer or Pension Officer.
